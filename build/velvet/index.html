Velvet

By Graham & Spencer

Interactive Design

Velvet by Graham and Spencer came to DFM looking to move its existing e-commerce business in-house. DFM was asked to design and build a multi-store website for sister brands Velvet and Graham & Spencer. Using the popular Magento e-commerce software, DFM designed both stores as a complement to each existing brand story while adhering to directed design requirements. DFM supports Velvet on a daily basis by acting as its creative department headed up by Velvet's in-house Art Director.
