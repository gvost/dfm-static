Modern web design is a complex composition of business strategy, marketing, and system integration, cloaked in imagery that communicates to a specific audience. DFM brings approachable creative vision to the process and provides insight, direction, and experience to the entrepreneur and executive team.

Websites are like paintings, but the paint never dries. They are constantly changing and being reworked. For most start-ups and small business, budget requires these changes to be done in-house. DFM provides the tools to effectively manage and build a brand on-line. In addition, DFM supports enterprise level clients on a monthly basis.
