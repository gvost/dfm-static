Branding

Premium rum brand Selvarey approached DFM with the challenge of creating a brand voice using existing artwork and ideas previously established by another design agency. DFM successful crafted a unique story focusing on the urban jungle and translated it to all brand aspects including the website, packaging, trade show, brand photography, and promotional merchandise.
